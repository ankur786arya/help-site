Scrumban Kickstart - Applying Scrumban to Optimize Lean Agility


This workshop is for teams that are already practicing Scrum and want to accelerate their progress towards becoming lean, agile machines! For every organization that has adopted Scrum, you can be certain it has also created its own unique adaptations. The Kanban Method recognizes this, and believes unique situations deserve the opportunity to further develop and refine their own process definitions -- definitions optimized to their domain, their workflow, the risks they manages, the skills of their teams, and the demands of their customers. [ read more about Scrumban ] In this workshop, participants will learn how to apply principles of Scrumban to optimize your Scrum environment, and how to best use ScrumDo's special features and functionality to this purpose. By the end of the workshop, the participating team will be able to:
  • Understand the differences between time boxed project and flow based projects
  • Visualize its ongoing work.
  • Visualize its current workflow (the way it works).
  • Familirize with commonly used practiced in flow based projects
  • Visualize its current policies (socially enforced principles that govern how it works).
  • Understand the use of Flow based metrics like Cumulative Flow Diagram, Flow Efficiency and Lead Time histograms
These outcomes will begin providing the team with enough information to understand it's current state, improve its Scrum going forward.
