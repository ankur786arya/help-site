Scrum Methodology in a Nutshell

Scrum is an iterative and incremental process for building software. Development teams commit to completing a certain amount of work within a time-boxed sprint. At the end of each sprint, the team reviews its work with a Product Owner, and holds a retrospective to identify how they can improve.

THE PROCESS IN A NUTSHELL
PRODUCT OWNER SCRUM TEAM SCRUM MASTER
  • Product Owner
    • Sets Priorities and Manages the Product Backlog
      Facilitate Product Owner involvement by making all the information they need to work with easy to access, prioritize, update and communicate about.
  • Scrum Team
    • Develops the Product and Self-organizes
      Keep your team motivated and productive by minimizing change. You're already starting a new process, so leverage your team's existing familiarity with tools and platforms you're already using.
  • Scrum Master
    • Manages the Process and Removes Impediments
      Maximize your Scrum Master's impact by employing applications that make it easy to monitor progress and automate the calculation of key metrics.
